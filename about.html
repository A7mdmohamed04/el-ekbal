About Ekbal Schools

El Ekbal schools represent a giant educational institution. It has engraved its name with letters of light among the greats and firsts at the level of Alexandria schools. Founded by the venerable judge, Counselor Nabih Zahran, it has become a pioneering icon in the distinguished education of the poor and the children of the middle class.

El Ekbal College schools in Alexandria embody the ideal model that deserves to be studied and replicated. With a distinguished work team of teachers, administrators, and parents, sponsored by a great leader, Counselor Nabih Zahran, El Ekbal has carved a big name in national schools.

Located on El Ekbal Street, next to Victoria College, with another branch on the roundabout containing a complex of schools (Arabic and languages), as well as another school for girls called (Zahraa El Ekbal), El Ekbal has truly become an educational citadel, a beacon of knowledge among the national schools of our beloved Egypt.

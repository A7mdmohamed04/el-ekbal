About Ekbal Schools

El Ekbal schools represent a giant educational institution. It engraved Its name with letters of light among the greats and firsts at the level of Alexandria schools. It was founded by the venerable judge, Counselor Nabih Zahran, the son of origins and nobility.It has become a pioneering icon in the distinguished education of the poor and the children of the middle class. The schools of El-Ekbal College in Alexandria are a model that embodies the story of The success of Counselor Zahran, a distinguished work team, and a success battalion working in silence.El Ebal College schools in Alexandria embody the ideal model that deserves to be studied and replicated by a distinguished work team of teachers, administrators, and parents, sponsored by a great leader who has carved a big name in national schools, namely Counselor Nabih Zahran. El-Ebal College schools are a star that shines in the sky of our dear Egypt. El-Ebal College schools in Alexandria are located on El-Ebal Street, next to Victoria College. There is another branch on the roundabout that contains a complex of schools (Arabic and languages), as well as another school for girls called (Zahraa El-Ebal). El-Ebal has become an educational citadel, a beacon of knowledge among the national schools of our beloved Egypt.
